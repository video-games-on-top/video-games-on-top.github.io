Our chapters are the backbone of this movement. Each one is led by passionate young people who recruit local members, run events, raise funds, and create real, lasting change in their city.

01

Apply to Lead

Fill out a short application telling us about yourself, your school, and why you want to lead a chapter in your area.

02

Build Your Team

Recruit fellow students as chapter members — each filling roles like Finance, Outreach, Events, and Communications.

03

Launch & Impact

Run drives, host events, and connect with local shelters — with full support from the national Streets to Shelters team.
