Why does it matter?

Streets to Shelters provides essential supplies and support to those experiencing homelessness. We don't just provide items; we restore dignity through care kits that show our community truly cares.

Why we started?

Created by students driven by action, we moved past conversation to coordination. By organizing donation drives and building kits, we bridge the gap between student volunteers and local outreach programs.
