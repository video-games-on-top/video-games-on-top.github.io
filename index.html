Our Mission

We provide essential resources to homeless shelters through organized drives, strategic partnerships, and consistent youth leadership. We focus on measurable, long-term impact — not one-time gestures.

Why does it matter?

Streets to Shelters provides essential supplies and support. We don't just provide items; we restore dignity through care kits that show our community truly cares.

Why we started?

Created by students driven by action. We organize donation drives and build kits to bridge the gap between volunteers and local outreach programs.
