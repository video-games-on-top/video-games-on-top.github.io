Chapter Summary

Streets to Shelters provides opportunities for students to lead and engage with their local communities by creating chapters. These chapters build empathy and offer tangible support for individuals experiencing homelessness. By establishing a chapter, students can coordinate supply drives, develop community partnerships, and organize impactful outreach efforts. We are dedicated to restoring dignity and expanding opportunity through collective student action.

Start a Chapter

Streets to Shelters Chapters are student-led teams dedicated to serving their local communities with compassion and action. Each chapter organizes outreach efforts, builds partnerships, and provides support to individuals experiencing homelessness.

By starting a chapter, students can lead supply drives, organize community outreach events, and help create pathways from the streets to safe shelter. Together, we can restore dignity, expand opportunity, and strengthen communities one chapter at a time.
